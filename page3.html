My First Ever Live Show

Besides concerts, I've also attended a live show by TMG aka Cody Ko and Noel Miller. For anyone that loves comedy, I recommend seeing them if they ever visit again. I had been watching them for a while on Youtube, so getting to see them in person was 100% worth it. I ended up getting free tickets from a close friend and I was incredibly grateful towards her, because it was a great time. The show was mainly stand-up comedy, inside jokes, and skits. They're so much funnier in person and they truly make everyone in the audience feel like they're included. They would often pick a random person in the audience and do an improv skit with them. It was always hilarious to see the faces of the unsuspecting audience member getting chosen and having to go along with whatever they had planned.

Cody and Noel are both Canadian and make songs as well (that are kinda really good actually). In a nutshell, if you're interested in a good beat and a good joke, check them out!

Next time I attend a comedy or stand-up show, I'll post a little review here, so stay tuned post-covid!
