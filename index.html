The Story of an Avid Concert-Goer (pre-covid)

Top 3 Concerts I've Been To

Conan Gray

I got tickets to see Conan Gray for my best friend's birthday. The show took place at Rebel (great bathrooms by the way). He gave an amazing performance filled with familiar songs and lots of dancing. He played never-released songs as well as the classics from the Sunset Season album. In the end, he gave a huge shoutout to Canada and told us a heartwarming story. Overall, it was a night to remember and it definitely made my friend's birthday a lot more special.

Rex Orange County

I had wanted to see Rex Orange County so bad, but tickets were sold out. The day before the concert, a friend of mine that had a ticket decided not to go and gave me the ticket instead (which was so so lucky). My friends and I had balcony seats at Danforth Music Hall, but we were standing the whole time. Rex played an incredible show with songs from his Pony album and even old songs from Apricot Princess. Even after it ended, we were still listening to his songs on the subway ride.

Yungblud

Yungblud was by far my number one favourite concert ever. It was incredibly high energy and I somehow teleported from the middle back all the way to the front AND I ended up getting a picture with him at the end. He played at Danforth Music Hall, where I was in the mosh pit, jumping and singing my heart out. He played songs from his 21st Century Liability album and I had an amazing time from start to finish. If I could see him again in concert, I 100% would.
