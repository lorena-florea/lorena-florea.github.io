Meet the Author: Lorena Florea

Lorena is a 21-year-old girl who loves going to concerts (if you couldn't tell) and is commemorating her time spent there through this website. She wasn’t really allowed to go to concerts when she was younger, so as soon as she turned 18, it was time to jam. Lorena has been to 15 concerts and 1 live show in the span of 2 years! She had 2 concerts planned for the summer of 2020 but COVID had other plans, and they, unfortunately, got postponed. Her favourite type of music varies from the ’80s to alternative rock to pop-punk, and more. Hopefully, this website makes you feel as if you were there with her jamming out.

She’s currently studying Graphic Communications Management at Ryerson working towards a minor in Communication Design and a concentration in Packaging. Lorena is a driven individual that always keeps her head up even when things get rough with the help of music and good friends. Hope you enjoy this site as much as she enjoyed making it. Here’s to more concerts in the upcoming years! :)
